Hello,

I'm mostly self-taught, beginning early in high-school when I fell in love with the idea of coding video games. I taught myself Java and latter C/Objective-C so that I could publish my games on the App Store.

After high-school, I went straight into the CS program at Purdue. I Studied there for about 2 years before applying to App Academy, a coding bootcamp in SF. After their very intensive course, I was able to land a job at Google as a web developer for their AdWords platform.

A little over a year ago, I made the move to Denver to be closer to friends/family. I now work for Transamerica, where I've spent most of my time heading up the front-end for their public sites.
